## Key Observations

- **CFO is strong and accelerating:** $139.5B in FY2025, up from $46.3B in FY2021 — a 3x increase in 4 years.
- **CapEx is surging:** $131.8B in FY2025, nearly all driven by data center and AI infrastructure buildout. This is the largest CapEx spend of any company globally.
- **FCF squeezed by investment cycle:** Despite massive CFO growth, FCF dropped to just $7.7B in FY2025 due to the CapEx ramp. This is a deliberate investment choice, not an operational problem.
- **D&A lags CapEx:** D&A as a % of revenue (9.2%) trails CapEx (18.4%), meaning current CapEx will generate depreciation charges for years. This creates a temporary drag on reported earnings as well.
